WHY ARE LIFE SKILLS
ESSENTIAL?

Life skills are those useful skills that help you participate fully and effectively in your daily activities. Life skills develop your potential as a person and help you achieve your goals. Any skill that helps you accomplish tasks and drives you can be called a life skill. In order to live a complete and successful life, we should keep learning and developing essential life skills.
